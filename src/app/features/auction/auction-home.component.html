NOTES FOR TESTING

- This is Home view. After logging in with MetaMask Wallet, please choose your prefered view from navigation.

    - Seller View | You act as a seller. Allows you to declare meter data (local browser storage), create auctions and view auctions you created.

    - Buyer View | You act as a buyer. Allows you to view all created auctions. In this view you can open any active auction and place your bid.
    (!!! WARNING !!! Choose different MetaMask account (connect different wallet address) to act as a buyer. Auction owners can't Bid.)

- Demo Auction List View | This view shows only successful auctions (status: Ended). This view shows FINAL deals between sellers and buyers.

- When testing, try to use couple different MetaMask wallets (Accounts) to see how app components are displayed based on your role (seller, buyer or passive participant).
